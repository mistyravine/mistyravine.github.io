About

October 18, 2024


    In quiet shadows lived one who floated between isolation and contemplation. A wanderer in thought, the company of silence was more pleasant for him than the noise of a world too loud to bear. Yet, his attempts to blend in were resisted-not from the world, but from within. Weary of trying, he erects a refuge for himself: an unconquerable fortress of solitude wherein his thoughts, his memories, and his quiet yearnings may breathe. Here, he reflects upon all that he holds dear, all which he loves, and those words which appear to slip away just when they are most required.

    Life is never easy, and the world-crueler on many an occasion-leaves its mark. But he endures. If he rises above to triumph or tumbles into the unknown, he faces it all with the resolve that is endemic in his demeanor, embracing whatever fate may come to his doorstep. This is his journey. It may not follow the well-trodden path, but it's his alone, and that's enough.

    Love can still find him, though he no longer expects it to be his savior. It's not about hoping for a rescue anymore; it's about the journey and accepting whatever the journey might be.
